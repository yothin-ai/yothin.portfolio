KMW Report

UX/UI for an internal financial reporting dashboard — designed for data clarity, role-based access, and a responsive layout that works across devices.

Requirement

  • Record every report in one systemCapture financial transactions, raw material usage, and other business data systematically.
  • Retrieve data fast, and keep it secureLet users pull up past reports quickly, with strong security built in.
  • Control access by roleGive executives full visibility while keeping employee access scoped to what they need.
  • Import and export with easeBring in data from Excel or CSV, and export reports as PDF or other formats.
  • Track deadlines automaticallyRemind users of submission deadlines and flag data that needs review.

Objective

  • Improve data accuracyCut errors and manual paperwork, and keep records precise.
  • Save time on everyday tasksLet employees record and retrieve data without redundant work.
  • Streamline internal data managementKeep storage and retrieval organized, with no lost documents.
  • Support smarter decisionsGive the business summary and analytical tools for planning.

Goal

  • Become the company's core reporting toolMake report management a standard, company-wide process.
  • Minimize human errorBuild accuracy into recording and calculations.
  • Boost operational efficiencyCut the time spent generating reports and finding data.
  • Scale with the businessSupport growth in data volume and users as the business expands.
