JETLAG

UX/UI for a food review platform across web and mobile — helping people discover restaurants and share honest dining experiences.

Requirement

  • Let users review honestlyRate, review, and share opinions about food and restaurants.
  • Rank by what mattersEstablishments ranked by quality, taste, service, and ambiance.
  • Surface the right detailsLocation, hours, and menu items with imagery.
  • Make discovery easySearch restaurants by type, price, or distance.
  • Build a followingLet users follow reviewers or favorite restaurants.

Objective

  • Reduce bad dining choicesAggregate real feedback to guide decisions.
  • Build communityCenter the platform around shared food experiences.
  • Support restaurantsGive them a channel for visibility and promotion.
  • Simplify decisionsMake picking where to eat fast and confident.

Goal

  • Become the go-to discovery appBe the first place people check before eating out.
  • Drive consistent reviewsKeep the review loop active and growing.
  • Connect restaurants and dinersEnable a direct relationship between the two.
  • Build for the long runArchitecture that supports future feature growth.
