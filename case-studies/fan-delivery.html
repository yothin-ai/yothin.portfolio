Fan Delivery

Full UX/UI for a food delivery app across web and mobile — from user journey mapping and wireframes to high-fidelity design, validated with feasibility reviews alongside the dev team.

Requirement

  • Order food in a few tapsLet users browse and order from favorite restaurants quickly, without friction.
  • Track orders in real timeShow progress from kitchen to doorstep, so users always know where their food is.
  • Match orders to the nearest riderConnect restaurants and delivery partners efficiently to keep delivery times short.
  • Support many ways to payAccept credit/debit cards, e-Wallets, QR codes, and cash on delivery.
  • Let users rate every orderKeep restaurants and riders accountable through ratings and reviews.

Objective

  • Make ordering effortlessGive customers a smooth, reliable experience anytime, anywhere.
  • Help restaurants sell moreGive partners an efficient channel to reach more customers and grow revenue.
  • Help riders earn moreBuild a flexible system so delivery partners can work and earn on their own terms.
  • Raise the bar for delivery serviceKeep improving service quality to stay ahead in a competitive market.

Goal

  • Become a leading delivery platformBe the first choice for customers ordering food.
  • Build a strong restaurant and rider networkKeep food vendors and delivery partners coordinated and efficient.
  • Grow customers and order volumeUse promotions and a seamless experience to attract and retain users.
  • Scale for what's nextSupport growth with subscriptions, loyalty perks, and pre-orders.
