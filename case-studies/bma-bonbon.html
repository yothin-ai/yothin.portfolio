RESPONSIVE WEB · UX/UI DESIGN

BMA บ่นบ่น

UX/UI for a mental wellness platform built around emotional support and community — designed to feel accessible, warm, and trustworthy in a sensitive context.

Requirement

  • Give people a safe space to open upLet those facing sadness or distress share their feelings without fear of judgment.
  • Protect anonymity by defaultLet users express themselves without revealing their identity.
  • Connect people to support that helpsPair peer support from people with similar experiences with guidance from certified psychologists and counselors.
  • Screen content to keep the space safeCatch harmful content, like self-harm encouragement or toxic language, before it spreads.

Objective

  • Make space for emotional releaseLet those who feel alone or unheard express themselves without criticism.
  • Build an empathetic communityEncourage people to share experiences and support each other.
  • Connect users to the right supportLink them to mental health resources and professional counseling.

Goal

  • Ease loneliness and emotional distressBecome a resource people turn to for relief and real solutions.
  • Raise awareness for mental healthShow the value of well-being and community support.
  • Build a safe, reliable platform for everyoneKeep the space secure and dependable for all users.
